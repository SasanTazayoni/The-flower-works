Frequently asked questions

Question: What types of flowers are in season right now?

Answer: Currently, we have a beautiful selection of roses, lilies, sunflowers, and dahlias, among others. Our inventory changes with the seasons, so we always offer fresh and in-season blooms.

Question: Can you create custom floral arrangements for special occasions?

Answer: Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom floral designs tailored to your specific preferences and occasions. Whether it's a wedding, anniversary, or birthday, we'll work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

Question: Do you provide delivery services?

Answer: Yes, we offer convenient delivery services to bring our floral arrangements directly to your desired location. Whether it's a residence, office, or event venue, our reliable delivery team ensures your flowers arrive fresh and on time.

Question: Can you help me choose flowers for a sympathy arrangement?

Answer: We understand the sensitivity of expressing condolences. Our compassionate florists will assist you in selecting appropriate flowers and creating tasteful sympathy arrangements that convey your heartfelt sentiments.

Question: Do you offer wedding consultations?

Answer: Absolutely! We provide personalized wedding consultations to discuss your floral needs, style preferences, and budget. We'll guide you through the process and create stunning floral arrangements that perfectly complement your special day.

Question: What other gift items do you offer besides flowers?

Answer: Alongside our floral arrangements, we offer a selection of complementary gift items. These include greeting cards, chocolates, stuffed animals, balloons, and more, allowing you to create a thoughtful and complete gift package.

Question: Can you provide floral decorations for corporate events?

Answer: Certainly! We specialize in floral decorations for corporate events. Our skilled team can design arrangements that align with your company's branding, creating a professional and welcoming ambiance for your event.

Question: How often should I water my potted plants?

Answer: The watering requirements vary for different types of plants. Our knowledgeable staff can provide specific care instructions for each plant you purchase, guiding you on watering frequency, sunlight exposure, and overall plant care.

Question: Do you offer seasonal discounts or promotions?

Answer: Yes, we often have seasonal promotions and discounts available. Be sure to check our website or visit our store to stay updated on our current offers and special deals.

Question: Can I order flowers in advance for a specific date?

Answer: Absolutely! We encourage customers to place advance orders for special occasions. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or holiday celebration, we'll ensure your flowers are prepared and delivered on the specified date, so your surprise is perfectly timed.
